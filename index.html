Model S

The Burninator

High Performance Sedan

Accelerates from 0-60 in 3.8s, while making almost no noise, and burning no fossil fuels.

At the same time, can seat 5 adults + 2 children, pushing the total cabin capacity to 7 people.

Long Distance-Ready

Tesla's Supercharger network makes it easy to take your Tesla on trips. Simply input a destination, and the NAV system will direct you to where you need to charge and for how long. Travel to nearly every part of the country for free and at almost no additional time to your trip.

Because there is no engine, a huge front trunk, or 'frunk', allows the Model S to have as much storage as many small SUVs, and far more storage than most sedans.

Future Tech, Today

Autopilot enables the car to nearly drive itself on highways by handling the acceleration / braking / lane-keeping, all while maintaining a safe distance and even avoiding other drivers.

The 17" Touchscreen provides access to everything on the vehicle easily, while enabling new features through over-the-air updates.

Safety

The Model S is the safest vehicle on the road today and it exceeds 5 stars in every safety category. More notably, in addition to failing to rotate in the standard roll-over test the Model S was able to withstand more top-down crush pressure than the testing equipment could exert; the equilevent to more than three Model S sedans stacked on the roof.

Performance

The version of Model S you are sitting in is one of the slow ones. The just released P100D is now tied as one of the three quickest accelerating production cars ever produced, hitting 0-60 in 2.5s, accelerating at over 1.1Gs, and continuing on through the 1/4 mile in 10.8s
