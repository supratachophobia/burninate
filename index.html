Model S

The Burninator

High Performance Sedan

Accelerates from 0-60 in 3.8s, while making almost no noise, and burning no fossil fuels.

At the same time, can seat 5 adults + 2 children, pushing the total cabin capacity to 7 people.

Long Distance-Ready

Tesla's Supercharger network makes it easy to take your Tesla on trips. Simply input a destination, and the NAV system will direct you to where you need to charge and for how long. Travel to nearly every part of the country for free and at almost no additional time to your trip.

  • 50% charge in 20min
  • 80% charge in 45min
  • 100% charge in 1hr

And because there is no engine, the front trunk, or 'frunk', allows the Model S to have as much total cargo space as many small SUVs, and far more storage than most sedans.

Future Tech, Today

Autopilot enables the car to nearly drive itself on highways by handling the acceleration / braking / lane-keeping. All this while maintaining a safe distance and even avoiding potentially dangerous manuevers of other drivers.

The 17" Touchscreen provides easy access to all the vehicles features and settings, leaving your dashboard free of buttons, knobs, and switches. Over-the-air updates continually improve the interface and feature-set of the car. Have an idea of something that could make your car better for you and other Tesla owners? Let Tesla know and your suggestion could make it into the next release!

Safety

The Model S is the safest vehicle on the road today, exceeding 5 stars in every safety category. In addition to failing to rotate in the standard roll-over test, the Model S was able to withstand more top-down crush pressure than the testing equipment could exert; the equilevent to more than three Model S sedans stacked on the roof.
